# Area Todo App

Area Todo is a location-aware todo list that lets users filter and sort their todos based on their location.

## Key Features

- A mobile-first web frontend
- The ability to find tasks that you need to do by proximity
- Clean UI indended for touchscreen interfaces like phones and tablets.
- The ability to have login sessions that are pinned to an area (e.g. a desktop computer can be logged in and you can set the location for that login as your office)

## Target Users

- People who struggle with remembering what they need to do when they get places
- People who may recieve tasks for work while not at work that they may need to remember the next day
- People who like breaking tasks down into smaller chunks
